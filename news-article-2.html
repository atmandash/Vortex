AI Diagnostic Tool Wins TIME's Best Invention

Nov 15, 2025 By Tech Reporter 3 min read

CHICAGO (Nov 15, 2025) — Prenosis Inc.'s revolutionary diagnostic tool, the Sepsis ImmunoScore™, has been named one of TIME Magazine's "Best Inventions of 2025." This FDA-authorized tool uses artificial intelligence to predict the onset of sepsis hours before traditional clinical symptoms become apparent.

Sepsis is notoriously difficult to diagnose in its early stages because its symptoms—fever, confusion, and rapid heart rate—can look like many other conditions. However, every hour of delayed treatment increases the risk of death by up to 8%.

How It Works

The Sepsis ImmunoScore™ integrates biological markers (blood tests) with clinical data (vitals) into a proprietary machine-learning algorithm. It generates a "risk score" that categorizes patients into varying levels of sepsis risk, allowing doctors to triage more effectively.

"This isn't just about faster diagnosis; it's about precision," said the CEO of Prenosis. "Our AI sees patterns in the immune response that a human eye simply cannot detect on a standard chart."

Hospitals that have piloted the tool report a 20% reduction in sepsis-related mortality and a significant decrease in unnecessary antibiotic use. The tool is now rolling out to major healthcare systems across the nation.
