Privacy Policy

1. Local-First Data Processing

We respect your privacy. This application is designed to run locally in your browser. Calculations for sepsis risk scores (qSOFA) are performed entirely on your device using Client-Side JavaScript. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal health information (PHI) to any external servers for analysis.

2. Data Collection Usage

  • Registration/Login: Basic credentials (phone number/ID) are stored in your browser's LocalStorage or a local demonstration database to maintain session state.
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track individual user behavior or diagnosis patterns.

3. Security

Since data resides on your local machine, security depends on your device's integrity. We recommend using SEPLUS on secure, private networks and devices.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to clear your data at any time by clearing your browser cache or LocalStorage. No data remains on our side because we never received it.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@seplus.org or visit us at VIT Chennai.
