Who We Are

We are a multidisciplinary team of developers, clinicians, and data scientists united by a single goal: fighting sepsis.

SEPLUS was born out of a hackathon challenge to create a lightweight, effective clinical decision support tool that works in low-resource settings. Today, it serves as a critical first line of defense in hospitals and clinics worldwide.
